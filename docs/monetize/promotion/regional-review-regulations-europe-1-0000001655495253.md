# 区域审核规范（欧洲）

<strong>Petal Ads Industry Admission Policy</strong>

<strong>In addition to the specific advertising policies of Petal Ads, your ads content and your use of Petal Ads services, must abide by all Applicable laws and regulations, including the laws and regulations of each country/market where you deliver the ads.</strong>

<strong>Your ad content and your use of Petal Ads services may not violate the intellectual property rights and the rights of publicity of any party.</strong>

<strong>All claims made in your ads content must be adequately substantiated.</strong>

<strong>Ads content may not be misleading.</strong>

<strong>Transparency requirements: All ads must have an “Ad” mark so end-users can identify the ads as paid placements.</strong>

| <strong>Dating websites and Apps ; marriage agencies</strong> | Advertising for dating services and/or marriage agencies may be prohibited or limited in some markets, subject to the local regulations.  Dating and marriage agencies ads must target people at least 18 years old or the legal age of majority in the location being targeted.  Ads not allowed:  - Content promoting services offering transactional relationships (e.g. mail-order bride and/or exchange of money or gifts) or promoting infidelity (e.g. affairs, model based on marital infidelity, etc.).  - Content promoting sexual transactions and/or encounters (e.g. hook-ups).  - Ads containing images that are pixelated or blurred.  - Ads must not include gender stereotypes that are likely to cause harm, or serious or widespread offence.  - Advertisers should give users some advice on precautions to take when meeting people through an advertisement for a dating or marriage agency.  – Content indicating that someone has searched for or will search for a user through promoted services, for example, "Five ladies have searched for you!" |

The ad content should not take advantage of consumers' inexperience or credulity.   - Ads content must make clear that the value of investments is variable and, unless guaranteed, can go down as well as up. If the value of the investment is guaranteed, the ad material must explain the guarantee.   - Content cannot publicize short-term personal loans (the repayment period does not exceed 60 days). Content should not disclose fraudulent information that may exist in mortgages or property sales.  <strong>All Expenses (such as the annual interest rate, handling fee, and interest rate on the landing page) and real addresses of the entities that issue products must be clearly and fully disclosed for promoting credit cards or financial services recognized by official institutions.</strong>  Ads shouldn’t target minors and products are promoted only to people aged 18 years or over based on each country minimum legal age requirement;  Content promoting credit cards, loans, or insurance services must not directly require users to enter personal financial information, including credit card information. Content shall not promote misleading or deceptive student loan mergers, exemptions, or restructuring services.
