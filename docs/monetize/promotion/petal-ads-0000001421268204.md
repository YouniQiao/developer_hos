# HUAWEI Ads is now Petal Ads

## Introducing Petal Ads

Say hello to a new era in smart advertising.

![](./img/zh-cn_image_0000001472755933_b0544a01c43a.png "点击放大")

We are excited to announce the next chapter for HUAWEI Ads platform. Starting in December, HUAWEI Ads will become Petal Ads. Since 2017, we have worked closely with advertising, media and publishing partners to create an expansive ads ecosystem.

Together, we have grown HUAWEI Ads into a vibrant marketplace with <strong>over 4 billion daily ad requests</strong> and <strong>integrated with over 360,000 global publishers</strong> and ad partners.

We are proud of how the platform has grown and <strong>evolved to serve customers beyond HUAWEI devices</strong>. We feel now is the right time to reflect the growth of the ecosystem and begin a new era as Petal Ads. We'd like to thank all our partners for their valuable support and look forward to growing the platform together in 2023.

![](./img/zh-cn_image_0000001422437270_5fd2d2c42023.gif)

<strong>Petal Ads: building a platform for growth and sustainability</strong>

Innovation is part of the DNA of all Petal products. Over the last few years, services such as Petal Search, Petal Maps and Petal Clip have brought more choice and open capabilities to the market. We will be taking all of those technical advances in AI powered mobile search, location-based services and creative editing tools to further power the growth of the Petal Ads platform.

While ‘Petal’ can be seen as strong symbol for growth, it is also a symbol for rejuvenation. We are committed to leading the way in sustainability in digital advertising. By leveraging our learnings in all-scenario products & experiences, we hope to work alongside our partners to build an ecosystem where revenue growth and sustainability go hand in hand.

![](./img/zh-cn_image_0000001473301981_c270f78fe326.png "点击放大")

<strong>2023 will see a new era for Petal Ads</strong>

Today we are surrounded by change. Consumers are shifting the way they search for information online, with mobile virtual assistants playing an increasingly important role in how people and businesses connect. Advances in big data, AR and machine learning are giving advertisers powerful tools to engage their audiences across devices. Through all this innovation and change, protecting the safety and security of our customers and partners remains our top priority.

Like the petals of a flower, it is only when all sides of the advertising ecosystem come together in perfect harmony, that something special emerges. Our mission for Petal Ads, is to be a connector in a sea of possibility. By working together with media partners, publishers, advertisers, MMPs and Industry bodies, we will continue to bring innovative ad experiences to all smart device users around the world.

## FAQS

<strong>Why has HUAWEI Ads platform changed its name to Petal Ads?</strong>

HUAWEI Ads has changed its name to Petal Ads to mark the evolution of the platform go beyond HUAWEI devices. As of Dec 2022, we have integrated with over 360,000 global publishers and ad partners, with more expansion planned for 2023.

<strong>When will this brand update take effect?</strong>

This brand update will be rolled out across the ads interface and marketing materials starting in December 2022.

<strong>Will my ad account be affected?</strong>

There is no change to existing terms of service or billing process for ad accounts. Any new partnership agreements going forward will refer to product name as 'Petal Ads' instead of 'HUAWEI Ads'.

<strong>Does the brand name change impact any existing agreements or contracts?</strong>

All existing agreements and contracts will remain valid.

<strong>Why has the platform changed its name?</strong>

The platform has changed its name to Petal Ads to mark the evolution of platform to go beyond HUAWEI devices, and reflect the growth of ecosystem to integrate over 360,000 global publishers and ad partners.

<strong>What should I do if I have more questions？</strong>

If you have any additional questions you can contact your business account representative or reach out to the Petal Ads team on petalads@huawei.com
