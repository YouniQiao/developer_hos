元服务音视频的开发方式与应用音视频的开发方式相同，针对元服务，Media Kit（媒体服务）当前仅开放支持以下功能：

* [音频播放](/docs/dev/app-dev/media/media-kit/media-kit-dev-arkts/media-playback-arkts/using-avplayer-for-playback)
* [音频录制](/docs/dev/app-dev/media/media-kit/media-kit-dev-arkts/media-recording-arkts/using-avrecorder-for-recording)
* [视频播放](/docs/dev/app-dev/media/media-kit/media-kit-dev-arkts/media-playback-arkts/video-playback)
